Sherlock

A modern update finds the famous sleuth and his doctor partner solving crime in 21st century London.
A smart and fresh take on the classic story. A great adaptation which translated the characters into modern age superbly. The story will have you hooked from the start and it's great that you don't need to know anything about the source material to understand it. It's a unique show well worth watching.
Best quote:
Mycroft Holmes(Mark Gatiss): "All lives end; all hearts are broken. Caring is not an advantage, Sherlock."

Dark

A family saga with a supernatural twist, set in a German town, where the disappearance of two young children exposes the relationships among four families.
A mind-bending show with a different approach to time-travel. An engaging experience from start to finish and every episode leaves you wanting to watch more. Pro tip: remember the characters names.
Best quote:
H.G. Tannhaus 1986(Christian Steyer): "What we know is a drop. What we don't know is an ocean."

Breaking bad

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine in order to secure his family's future.
Beyond briliant, this show offers everything a good show needs to offer. It has one of the best character arc of all times. Don't watch it because no other show will ever come close to this one.
Best quote:
Skyler White(Anna Gunn): "If I have to hear one more time that you did this for the family..."
Walter White(Bryan Cranston): "I did it for me. I liked it. I was good at it. And... I was really... I was alive."
