Blade Runner 2049

Thirty years after the events of Blade Runner (1982), a new Blade Runner, L.A.P.D. Officer "K" (Ryan Gosling), unearths a long-buried secret that has the potential to plunge what's left of society into chaos. K's discovery leads him on a quest to find Rick Deckard (Harrison Ford), a former L.A.P.D. Blade Runner, who has been missing for thirty years.
Even if it's often overlooked by the general audience, this movie is one of my favourites. Mostly famed for it's breathtaking visuals, Blade runner 2049 has an incredible story and great performances. Even though it has a running time of 2 hours and 44 minutes, you definitely won't be bored. But before watching this movie, you should know that this is a sequel to Blade Runner(1982).
Best quote:
Joi(Ana de Armas): "Mere data makes a man. A and C and T and G. The alphabet of you. All from four symbols. I am only two: 1 and 0."
'K'(Ryan Gosling): "Half as much but twice as elegant, sweetheart."

Howl's moving castle

When an unconfident young woman is cursed with an old body by a spiteful witch, her only chance of breaking the spell lies with a self-indulgent yet insecure young wizard and his companions in his legged, walking castle.
Beautiful and surreal, this is a movie everyone should see at least once. Make some popcorn, take a blanket, sit on the couch and enjoy 2 hours of a beautiful animation, interesting story and great characters.
Best quote:
Howl(Christian Bale): "I feel terrible, like there's a weight on my chest."
Young Sophie(Emily Mortimer): "A heart's a heavy burden."

Interstellar

Earth's future has been riddled by disasters, famines, and droughts. There is only one way to ensure mankind's survival: Interstellar travel. A newly discovered wormhole in the far reaches of our solar system allows a team of astronauts to go where no man has gone before, a planet that may have the right environment to sustain human life.
Emotional and smart, this movie really is an unforgetable experience. With an awesome story and breathtaking imagery, it makes you feel like you've been in space with the characters. It has great performances, script and direction, but by far the best part is Hans Zimmer's music. A film for all ages.
Best quote:
Cooper(Matthew McConaughey): "We used to look up at the sky and wonder at our place in the stars. Now we just look down, and worry about our place in the dirt."
